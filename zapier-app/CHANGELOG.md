## 1.0.0

Initial release to public.

Triggers:
- New Lead (instant via REST hook + 15-minute polling fallback)
- New Contact (instant + polling)
- Tag Added to Contact (instant)
- Proposal Signed (instant + polling)
- Payment Received (instant + polling)
- Appointment Booked (instant + polling)

Actions (creates):
- Create or Update Contact
- Create Lead
- Add Tag to Contact
- Send SMS
- Send Email

Searches:
- Find Contact by Email
