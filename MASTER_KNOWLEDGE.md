## WHAT STORYVENUE IS

StoryVenue is an all-in-one proposal, payment, and client management platform built exclusively for wedding venues. It replaces the patchwork of spreadsheets, PDF contracts, Venmo payments, and scattered email threads that most venue owners juggle daily. A venue owner sends one branded link. The client reviews the proposal, signs the contract, and pays — all in a single flow, on any device.

**Tagline options:**
- Send. Sign. Get Paid. One Link.
- The wedding venue platform that closes deals for you.
- Proposals, contracts & payments — one link, done.
- Stop chasing payments. Start closing deals.

## THE PROBLEM (USE IN ADS & SALES COPY)

Wedding venue owners are event professionals, not bookkeepers or sales managers. They're running tastings, coordinating with florists, managing staff — not sitting behind a desk chasing signatures and payments. Here's what they deal with every week:

**1. The PDF Dance** — They create a proposal in Word or Canva, export a PDF, email it, wait days, get a reply asking for changes, re-export, re-send, wait again. Average close time: 5-14 days.

**2. The Payment Chase** — After finally getting a signed contract, they send a Venmo link or invoice and wait. Some couples pay immediately. Others forget. The owner texts, emails, texts again. Average time to collect deposit: 7-21 days after signing.

**3. Lost Leads** — An inquiry comes in through The Knot or a website form. The owner sees it hours later, responds, doesn't hear back, and moves on. No follow-up system. No tracking. The lead is gone.

**4. Spreadsheet Chaos** — Client info lives in a Google Sheet, contracts in Google Drive, payment records in Venmo/PayPal, conversations in text messages and email. Nothing connects. When a bride calls asking about her balance, the owner scrambles across 4 apps to find the answer.

**5. No Visibility** — How many leads came in this month? What's the pipeline value? Which proposals are outstanding? Who hasn't paid? Most venue owners have no idea. They're flying blind.

**6. Zero Follow-Up Automation** — After a tour, the owner means to follow up. But Saturday is a wedding day, Sunday is recovery, Monday is admin chaos. By Tuesday, the lead has toured two other venues and the moment is gone.

## COMPETITIVE POSITIONING

**vs. HoneyBook / Dubsado / Aisle Planner:**
- These are general event planning tools. They work for florists, photographers, DJs, AND venues. StoryVenue is built exclusively for venues — the terminology, workflows, and features are all venue-specific.
- Built-in AI Concierge that follows up with leads automatically. HoneyBook doesn't do this.
- Direct payment processing with installments and subscriptions. No third-party payment setup needed.
- Space-based calendar with conflict detection. General tools don't understand that a venue has multiple bookable spaces.

**vs. The Knot / WeddingWire:**
- These are lead generation platforms. They send you leads but don't help you close them. StoryVenue is what happens AFTER the lead comes in.
- Proposal → Sign → Pay in one link. The Knot doesn't do proposals or payments.
- AI follow-up that nurtures leads 24/7. WeddingWire sends you the lead and walks away.

**vs. Stripe / Square / PayPal:**
- These are payment processors. They don't create proposals, capture signatures, manage leads, or send follow-ups.
- StoryVenue includes payment processing AND everything around it.
- Installment plans and subscriptions are built into the proposal flow, not bolted on.

**vs. DocuSign / PandaDoc:**
- These are document signing tools. They don't process payments, manage leads, or send follow-up sequences.
- StoryVenue's e-signature is part of the proposal → sign → pay flow. One link, not three.
- No per-document fees. Unlimited proposals.

**vs. DIY Stack (Google Sheets + Venmo + Gmail + DocuSign):**
- Everything in one place instead of 4+ tools.
- Automated follow-up instead of manual texting.
- Financial reports instead of spreadsheet math.
- Professional branded experience instead of patchwork.

StoryVenue is built specifically for wedding venues. HoneyBook works for 50 different vendor types — we work for one. Our AI Concierge follows up with leads automatically. You can. And you'll spend 5 hours a week on admin that we automate. How much is your time worth? If you bill $150/hour for event coordination, that's $750/week — $3,000/month — in lost revenue from doing tasks that should be automated.

**"My clients are old-school. They want a PDF."**
Your clients are millennials and Gen Z booking weddings in 2025-2026. They live on their phones. They Venmo, Apple Pay, and tap-to-pay everywhere. A beautiful mobile-friendly proposal link is MORE accessible to them than a PDF attachment.

The AI doesn't replace you — it handles the follow-up you never get to. You're running a wedding on Saturday, recovering Sunday, buried in admin Monday. By Tuesday, the lead who toured your venue has toured two others. The AI texted them on Sunday. You're welcome.

**"I'm too small for this."**
If you book even 10 weddings a year at $5,000 each, that's $50,000 flowing through your process. One lost lead or one forgotten payment reminder costs you thousands. StoryVenue pays for itself with a single extra booking or a single deposit collected 2 weeks faster.

**"What if my data gets lost?"**
Your data lives in a production-grade PostgreSQL database with automated backups. It's more secure than the Google Sheet on your laptop.

**"Is my clients' payment info safe?"**
Payment processing is handled by LunarPay — a PCI-compliant payment processor. We never store credit card numbers. The same security standards that protect major e-commerce sites protect your transactions.
